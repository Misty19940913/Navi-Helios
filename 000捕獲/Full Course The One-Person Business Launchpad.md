### a beginner course to making your first $5k in the simplest way possible

**"I want to start a business, but which one do I start? And how do I start it?"**

I get it.

When you think of starting a business, so many questions start flooding in.

When I realized that a 9-5 wasn't for me, I did everything in my power to avoid getting one.

But that meant I didn't have an *employer to give me a paycheck every month. It was all on me now. Every part of the business. The thought of that overwhelmed me.*

Do I need money to start?

Wtf is an LLC?

Taxes? Oh god.

Can I do something related to my interests?

Or should I sell out now and try to make money fast?

I would latch onto any quick and "profitable" business model I could find.

I tried dropshipping.

I tried a Facebook ads agency.

I tried a content marketing and SEO agency.

I tried freelance photography and editing.

I tried digital art on social media.

I tried ordering my own blue light glasses and wallets to sell.

Each and every one of those businesses failed.

(Well, they didn't really fail, I just stopped.)

But the skills I stacked during that time made it click for me:

*All successful businesses have 2 things in common:*

**1) They have somewhere to generate traffic.**

This could be anything from social media to paid ads to podcast sponsorships or posting in Facebook groups.

**2) They have somewhere to host and accept payment for a product or service.**

This could be a Shopify store, an invoice software for freelance clients, or something like a course platform.

But that's still super broad and confusing.

*The question now is, what can you start doing right now.*

As one person.

Without investing your life savings.

In a way that works with any skill or interest…

And can be scaled as far as you want to go?

We don't live in the industrial age anymore.

There are more options than the outdated path of *"go to school, get a job, maybe start a local physical business that costs tens of thousands along the way, and retire at 65."*

You have access to the internet for goodness sake.

You have access to internet courses where you can learn almost anything much faster than you could with a traditional education.

And luckily, you're at that point in history where there are so many tools out there.

You can whip up a website with the click of a button.

(Which used to take 10 web developers to create).

You can post a random idea to social media and have 10,000 people see it (on the *very* low end).

And if you have a product or service, all it takes is having a tiny fraction of those people pay you for your skillset and knowledge.

And that's just one social post! Imagine if you had a streamlined system to focus on every morning.

**In The One-Person Business LaunchPad, we are focused on making your first $5K.**

That's getting a measly 5 people to purchase a $1000 service.

And yes, I help you create that too (based around your own skills and interests).

Here's what you receive that makes it very difficult to fail:

✅ The fastest way to setup your store, website, blog, and newsletter all at once.

✅ The fundamentals of online business. In other words, we answer the question, "How do I *actually* make money outside of a job?"

✅ The 3-point marketing strategy so you know exactly how to get customers (it's like a $40,000 marketing degree condensed into 3 sentences.)

✅ A one-page business plan so you don't get stuck after finishing this course.

✅ Learn a streamlined style of digital marketing that you can repeat over and over again (no matter what you decide to sell from freelance services to physical products or digital products).

✅ A complete breakdown of what to sell, how to sell it, and where to sell it (since the internet can get confusing).

✅ Make your first $5-$10K with a "micro offer" that you can start selling immediately.

✅ How to turn your interests into a valuable offer that people *want to pay you for.*

### And of course, a few bonuses:

⚡️ Templates to write your first 10 social posts, 3 threads, and 1 newsletter (that attract and gain customers).

⚡️ Templates for your marketing strategy and offer creation that you can refer to at any time.

⚡️ Tutorials and walkthrough of the software you will be using to accept payment for what you plan to sell.

All-in-all…

I created this course for my past self.

This is the course I wish I had so I didn't waste my time failing at 7 business models that didn't fulfill me.

This is what I would do if I wanted to fast-track my way to $5K and beyond.

If you want to stop living in your head wondering when and if you should start the damn thing, maybe now is the perfect opportunity.
